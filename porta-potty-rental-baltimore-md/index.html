Baltimore Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Maryland regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • 1
    Private Property Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit.
  • 2
    Right-of-Way Permits If placing a unit on a Baltimore public sidewalk or street, a temporary right-of-way permit from the City is mandatory.

    OSHA Construction Rules Commercial sites must have 1 toilet per 20 workers, and hand washing stations must be located adjacent to all portable restrooms.
