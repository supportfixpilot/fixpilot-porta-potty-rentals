Contracting workflow for military

  1. Contracting officer / KO issues solicitation or task order
  2. We respond with FAR-compliant proposal — pricing, capability statement, past performance, COI
  3. Award — CO issues SF-1449 or task order under existing IDIQ/BPA
  4. Pre-deployment coordination with COR (Contracting Officer’s Representative) for site, gate access, exercise schedule
  5. Delivery — pre-cleared trucks, sponsor escort if required for restricted areas
  6. Servicing during exercise on agreed schedule
  7. Pickup post-exercise; FAR-compliant invoice via WAWF or contract-specified method
