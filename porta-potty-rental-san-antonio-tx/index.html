Failing to provide proper sanitation can lead to massive OSHA fines and slowed productivity. We keep your San Antonio job site fully compliant.

1 Toilet Per 20 Workers

Federal OSHA guidelines require one clean, functional portable toilet for every 20 employees on a commercial construction site.

Mandatory Handwashing

If workers are exposed to hazardous materials or lead, dedicated hand washing stations with running water and soap are legally required.

Weekly Pumping Minimum

To maintain sanitary conditions in the Texas heat, we mandate a minimum of once-a-week servicing, pumping, and restocking for all long-term rentals.
