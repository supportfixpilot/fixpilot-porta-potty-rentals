# Google Business Profile Setup Guide for FixPilot

## Overview

A Google Business Profile (GBP) is critical for local SEO. When someone searches for "porta potty rental near me" or "porta potty [city name]", your GBP listing appears in the local 3-pack and Google Maps, driving phone calls and website visits.

## Why GBP Matters

- **Local 3-Pack Visibility**: Appear in Google's top 3 local results
- **Google Maps Integration**: Show up when people search "porta potty near [location]"
- **Customer Reviews**: Collect and display reviews that build trust
- **Business Information**: Share hours, services, photos, and contact info
- **Call Tracking**: Use unique phone numbers to track leads by city

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Create or Claim Your Business

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Click **"Manage Now"** or sign in to your Google account
3. Click **"Add your business to Google"**
4. Enter your business name: **FixPilot Porta Potty Rentals**
5. Choose the category: **Equipment Rental Company** (or "Portable Toilet Rental Service")

### Step 2: Enter Business Information

For each major city you serve, create a separate listing with the local address:

#### Example: Houston, TX
- **Business Name**: FixPilot Porta Potty Rentals - Houston
- **Address**: 4520 Griggs Rd, Houston, TX 77021
- **Phone**: (713) 555-PORT
- **Website**: https://fixpilotportapottyrentals.com/locations/houston/

#### Example: Dallas, TX
- **Business Name**: FixPilot Porta Potty Rentals - Dallas
- **Address**: 3100 Commerce St, Dallas, TX 75226
- **Phone**: (214) 555-PORT
- **Website**: https://fixpilotportapottyrentals.com/locations/dallas/

### Step 3: Verification

Google will send a **postcard** to the business address with a verification code (takes 5-14 days). Enter the code in your GBP dashboard to verify ownership.

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile

Fill out every field:

- **Hours**: Mon-Sat 7AM-6PM, Sun 8AM-4PM
- **Service Area**: List the cities/metros you serve
- **Categories**: 
  - Portable Toilet Rental Service
  - Equipment Rental Company
  - Construction Equipment Rental
- **Services**: List all your unit types
- **Photos**: Add photos of your units, trucks, team

### Step 5: Add Service Pages

Link each location page from your website to the corresponding GBP listing.

## Managing Multiple Locations

### Option A: Individual Accounts
Create separate Google accounts for each market manager to maintain local listings.

### Option B: Business Account Group
Use Google's multi-location management:
1. Create a Business Profile Group
2. Add all location listings
3. Manage centrally while allowing local access

### Option C: Third-Party Tools
Consider tools like:
- **Moz Local**: Distribute listings across directories
- **Yext**: Manage all locations from one dashboard

## Collecting Reviews

### Ask for Reviews
Train staff to ask satisfied customers:
- "Would you mind leaving us a review on Google? It really helps small businesses."
- Send follow-up emails with review links
- Include review request in receipts

### Respond to Reviews
- Thank customers for positive reviews
- Address negative reviews professionally
- Never argue or be defensive

### Review Templates

#### Request for Positive Review
```
Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing FixPilot for your porta potty rental! We're glad we could help with your [event/project].

If you had a great experience, we'd really appreciate it if you could take a moment to leave us a review on Google: [review link]

Your feedback helps other businesses find us. Thanks again!

Best,
The FixPilot Team
```

#### Response to Positive Review
```
Hi [Reviewer Name],

Thank you so much for the kind words! We really appreciate your business and are thrilled to hear you had a great experience with [service description].

We're always here if you need us again. Have a great [day/week]!

Best regards,
The FixPilot Team
```

#### Response to Negative Review
```
Hi [Reviewer Name],

We're sorry to hear about your experience. Your feedback is important to us, and we've shared your concerns with our team to make improvements.

We'd like to make this right. Please call us at [phone number] so we can discuss this further.

Sincerely,
The FixPilot Management
```

## Posting Updates

Use Google Posts to stay active:

### Types of Posts
- **What's New**: New service offerings
- **Events**: Community involvement
- **Offers**: Seasonal promotions
- **Products**: Featured unit types

### Post Frequency
- Post at least 1-2 times per month
- Include photos for engagement
- Add CTAs (Call Now, Learn More)

## Measuring Success

Track these metrics monthly:

- **Search Queries**: How people find you
- **Direction Requests**: Map navigation clicks
- **Phone Calls**: Call tracking
- **Website Clicks**: Traffic from GBP
- **Photos Views**: Engagement with your images
- **Reviews**: Quantity and rating average

## NAP Consistency

NAP (Name, Address, Phone) must be **identical** across all listings:

### Correct Format
```
FixPilot Porta Potty Rentals
4520 Griggs Rd
Houston, TX 77021
(713) 555-2683
```

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Using different phone numbers (use one main number or track with extensions)
- Varying address formats (always use full street address)
- Inconsistent business names (always use exact same name)
- Missing suite/unit numbers
