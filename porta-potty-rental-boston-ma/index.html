Boston DPW Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Boston's narrow streets and strict Department of Public Works regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit in Boston.
  • Right-of-Way Permits If placing a unit on a public sidewalk, street, or parking meter space, a temporary Street Occupancy Permit from the Boston DPW is mandatory.
  • OSHA Construction Rules Commercial sites must have 1 toilet per 20 workers, and hand washing stations must be located adjacent to all portable restrooms.
