Same-Day Porta Potty Rental: Is It Possible?

By Jordan Reed, Senior Sanitation Operations Manager · Reviewed by Priya Patel · Updated 2026-06-11
February 25, 2026 5 min read

Life happens. Sometimes you need emergency portable restrooms NOW. Here's the truth about same-day rentals and how to maximize your chances of getting what you need, when you need it.

The Short Answer: Yes, But...

Same-day delivery is possible but not guaranteed. Several factors determine whether we can fulfill a last-minute order:

  • Location: We have depots across the country, but coverage varies
  • Availability: Units may already be deployed
  • Type of unit: Standard units are more available than specialty units
  • Time of day: Earlier calls have better chances
  • Site accessibility: Delivery trucks need room to operate

Factors That Affect Same-Day Availability

Peak Season (Spring-Summer)

Demand spikes during warmer months. Construction season and outdoor events mean units are in high demand. Book at least 3-5 days in advance during these periods.

Weekend Crunch

Most events happen on weekends. Saturday requests are the hardest to fulfill. If your event is Saturday, call by Tuesday at the latest.

Local Demand

Big events in your area (concerts, festivals, sports tournaments) can wipe out local inventory. Check if there are major events happening near you.

How to Improve Your Chances

  1. 1
    Call early: First thing in the morning (7-8 AM) gives you the best selection
  2. 2
    Be flexible on delivery time: We can often fit you in during off-peak hours
  3. 3
    Consider alternatives: If you need 10 units, we might have 6 available now
  4. 4
    Ask about nearby locations: We can sometimes transfer units from nearby depots

Same-Day Fees

Be prepared for potential additional charges with emergency orders:

  • Rush delivery fee: $25-75 depending on distance
  • After-hours delivery: Additional charge for evening/weekend orders
  • Priority routing: May require premium pricing

What to Have Ready When You Call

Speed up the process by having this information ready:

  • Delivery address with gate access codes
  • Site conditions: Paved, dirt, slope?
  • Space for delivery truck: 12-foot wide, 40-foot long minimum
  • Crane or forklift needs: If units need to go somewhere unusual
  • Rental duration: How long you need them
  • Payment information: We'll need a credit card to hold the order

Same-Day Porta Potty Rental — Extended Guide

Construction Site Emergency

A porta potty tipping over at a construction site is a serious emergency that requires immediate attention. In this scenario, same-day delivery is highly likely to be successful, as porta potty rental companies understand the urgency of the situation and the importance of maintaining a safe and healthy working environment. The key to success lies in calling the rental company as soon as possible, explaining the situation, and providing the necessary details, such as the location and the number of units needed. When calling, say something like: "Hi, we have a porta potty emergency at our construction site. One of the units has tipped over, and we need a replacement as soon as possible. Can you please provide same-day delivery?"

Last-Minute Outdoor Birthday Party

Organizing a last-minute outdoor birthday party for 50 people can be challenging, especially when it comes to arranging for porta potty rentals. In this scenario, same-day delivery is possible but not guaranteed, as it depends on the rental company's availability and the location of the party. To maximize the chances of success, call the rental company early in the morning, explain the situation, and provide the necessary details, such as the location, number of guests, and desired delivery time. When calling, say something like: "Hi, I'm organizing a last-minute outdoor birthday party for 50 people, and I need to rent some porta potties. Can you please provide same-day delivery? I'd appreciate it if you could deliver the units by [time] to ensure everything is ready for the party."

Festival Organizer's Last-Minute Realization

Forgetting to book porta potty rentals is a mistake that can happen to even the most experienced festival organizers. In this scenario, same-day delivery is unlikely to be successful, as festival organizers typically require a large number of units, and rental companies may not have enough inventory available on short notice. However, it's still worth calling the rental company to explain the situation and provide the necessary details, such as the location, number of attendees, and desired delivery time. When calling, say something like: "Hi, I'm the organizer of [festival name], and I realized that I forgot to book porta potty rentals. I know it's last-minute, but I was wondering if you could provide same-day delivery? I understand if it's not possible, but I'd appreciate any help you can offer."

Film Production's Remote Location Shoot

Film productions often require porta potty rentals for remote location shoots, and same-day delivery may be necessary to meet tight filming schedules. In this scenario, same-day delivery is possible but requires careful planning and coordination with the rental company. To maximize the chances of success, call the rental company well in advance, explain the situation, and provide the necessary details, such as the location, number of crew members, and desired delivery time. When calling, say something like: "Hi, I'm the production manager for [film production], and we're shooting on location at [remote location]. We need to rent some porta potties for the crew, and we require same-day delivery to meet our filming schedule. Can you please provide the units and arrange for delivery by [time]?"
