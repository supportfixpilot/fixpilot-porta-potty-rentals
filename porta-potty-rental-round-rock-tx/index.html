Round Rock Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Williamson County and City of Round Rock regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit.
