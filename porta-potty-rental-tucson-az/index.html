Tucson Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Pima County regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit.
  • Right-of-Way Permits: If placing a unit on a Tucson public street, sidewalk, or alleyway, a temporary right-of-way permit from the City of Tucson is mandatory.
  • OSHA Construction Rules: Commercial sites must have 1 toilet per 20 workers, and hand washing stations must be located adjacent to all portable restrooms in Arizona.
