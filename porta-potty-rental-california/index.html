California-specific compliance & logistics

California work-site sanitation falls under Cal/OSHA Title 8 §1526 and §3366. Cal/OSHA's ratios are stricter than federal OSHA — our default California unit counts use the Cal/OSHA tables.

Construction sites

Weddings & events
