Bridgeport Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Fairfield County regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit.
  • Right-of-Way Permits: If placing a unit on a Bridgeport public sidewalk, street, or alleyway, an encroachment permit from the City is mandatory.
  • OSHA Construction Rules: Commercial sites must have 1 toilet per 20 workers, and hand washing stations must be located adjacent to all portable restrooms.

How to Prepare Your Site for Delivery

Ensure a smooth, hassle-free delivery in Bridgeport by following these three simple site preparation steps.

1

Clear the Path

Our delivery trucks need a minimum of a 10-foot wide clearance. Ensure gates are unlocked, vehicles are moved, and low-hanging branches are trimmed.

2

Level the Ground

Units must be placed on dry, level ground (pavement, gravel, or flat dirt). Sloped or severely muddy areas are unsafe for placement and servicing.

3

Service Access

For long-term rentals, our vacuum trucks must be able to park within 25 feet of the unit to perform the weekly pumping and cleaning safely.
