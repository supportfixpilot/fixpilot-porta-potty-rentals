Permitting in Atlanta for 30305

When placing a portable restroom unit on or adjacent to public property in Atlanta, the City of Atlanta requires an encroachment permit for any placement within the public right-of-way — including sidewalks, curb lanes, and city easements. This permit typically costs between $75 and $125 and requires approximately five business days for processing through the Atlanta Department of Public Works. Units placed entirely on private property, such as a construction site, parking lot, or residential yard set back from the public way, do not require a city permit. In gated residential communities and HOA-governed developments common throughout 30305, additional written approval from the homeowners association may be required before delivery.
