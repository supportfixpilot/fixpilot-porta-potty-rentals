Newark & NJ Permits for 07102 Units

Placing portable sanitation units in Downtown Newark's 07102 ZIP code involves layered permitting requirements that FixPilot navigates on behalf of clients. The City of Newark requires a Construction Board of Appeals filing for sanitation installations tied to public construction projects, ensuring compliance with site safety plans and zoning conditions. For any unit placed on a public right-of-way — sidewalks, street shoulders, or curb lanes — Essex County ROW permits are required and typically run between $75 and $200 depending on placement duration and street classification. On government-funded or public-employee jobsites, New Jersey PEOSH (Public Employees Occupational Safety and Health) standards govern sanitation ratios, mandating a minimum of one toilet per 15 workers.
