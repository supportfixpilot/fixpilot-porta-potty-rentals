What drives the price differences

  • Labor cost — California, Massachusetts, New York, Washington run higher because driver and disposal labor cost more.
  • Distance from depot — Rural states (Wyoming, Montana, Nevada interior) often have remote-site uplifts on top of base price.
  • Seasonal weather — Phoenix summer surcharge, Bakken winter additives, Florida hurricane tie-downs.
  • Permit complexity — NYC DOT, San Francisco SFMTA, DC DDOT permits are more expensive than typical.
  • Supply density — Texas and Florida have many porta-potty operators competing, which keeps prices lower than supply-tight markets.
  • Event-calendar surge — Las Vegas during conventions, Nashville on Saturdays, Austin during SXSW — all run premium pricing for those windows.
