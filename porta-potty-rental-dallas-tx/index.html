Dallas Site Compliance & Placement Guide

Running a construction site or event in the DFW Metroplex means adhering strictly to OSHA guidelines and local Dallas ordinances. We handle the logistics so you don't get hit with stop-work orders or fines.

Dallas Right-of-Way (ROW) Permits

If placing a porta potty on a public street, sidewalk, or alley (common in Deep Ellum or Uptown), a temporary ROW permit from the City of Dallas is mandatory. Units strictly on private property do not require this.

OSHA Sanitation Requirements

Commercial sites must provide 1 toilet for every 20 workers on site. Furthermore, OSHA requires standalone handwashing stations to be located immediately adjacent to the portable toilets.
