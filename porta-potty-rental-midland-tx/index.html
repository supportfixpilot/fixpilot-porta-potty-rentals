Midland Placement & OSHA Compliance Rules

Navigating Texas regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal and safe.

  • Private Property vs. Street: Units placed entirely within your property line or fenced job site do not require a street permit. If placed on a public right-of-way, City of Midland permits are mandatory.
  • OSHA Construction Rules: Commercial sites and oilfields must have 1 toilet per 20 workers. Hand washing stations must be located adjacent to all portable restrooms if plumbing is unavailable.
  • Environmental Safety: Holding tanks and portable units must be secured to prevent tip-overs during high West Texas winds, protecting the soil from contamination.
