Specialized Solutions for Remote Sites

Remote locations often require different equipment than standard event venues. At FixPilot, we offer specialized solutions designed for challenging environments:

Trailer-Mounted Porta Potties

Our trailer-mounted units are perfect for film sets and locations that change throughout the day. These mobile units can be towed to different locations on your site, providing flexibility for large areas or moving productions.

Off-Road Delivery

Our delivery vehicles can access many challenging locations. For extremely remote sites, we can coordinate helicopter delivery or use specialized tracking equipment to get units where they need to be.

Solar & Green Options

For environmentally sensitive locations, we offer solar-powered units and eco-friendly options that minimize environmental impact while still providing excellent sanitation.

Remote Servicing

We schedule regular servicing for long-term remote installations. Our service technicians are experienced in maintaining units at remote locations and will work with you to create a maintenance schedule that keeps your site clean and functional.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you deliver porta potties to a beach location?

Yes! We have experience delivering to beach locations and coastal areas. We use specialized equipment to navigate sand and soft ground conditions. Let us know the specific beach location and we'll work with you to determine the best delivery approach.

What units do you recommend for film sets?

For film productions, we typically recommend a combination of standard units for background actors and extras, plus deluxe units with sinks for principal actors and crew. Trailer-mounted units are also popular as they can be moved around the set as filming locations change.

Do you provide units for farmland agricultural work?

Absolutely! Farms and agricultural operations are a significant part of our business. We provide units for harvest season, farm construction projects, and agricultural events. Our units can handle the rough terrain often found on farms.

How do you service units in very remote locations?

For remote locations, we work with you to establish a servicing schedule that makes sense. For some extremely remote sites, we may coordinate with local pumping services. We'll discuss all options with you when you book to ensure your units stay clean throughout your rental period.
