How Many Porta Potties Do You Need?

Determining the right number of portable toilets for your sporting event is crucial. Too few units leads to long lines and unhappy guests; too many wastes money. Here's our general guideline:

Event Size Recommended Units Duration
50-100 guests 2-3 units 2-4 hours
100-250 guests 4-6 units 4-8 hours
250-500 guests 8-12 units All day
500-1000 guests 15-25 units All day
1000+ guests 25+ units Multi-day

* These are general estimates. Specific needs may vary based on event type, alcohol service, and other factors. Call us for a precise recommendation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you provide units for a marathon course?

Yes! We regularly serve marathon and long-distance running events. We can place units at the start/finish line and at water stations along the course. Just let us know your course layout and we'll create a placement plan.

Do you offer hand washing stations?

Absolutely! Hand wash stations are highly recommended for sporting events, especially those involving food service or when athletes need to clean up after activities. We offer standalone hand washing stations and hand sanitizer dispensers.

What about ADA-accessible units?

We provide ADA-compliant portable toilets that meet all federal accessibility requirements. These units are wider, have grab bars, and are on level ground. They're essential for inclusive events and often required by law.

Can you set up at youth sports complexes?

Yes! We have extensive experience with youth sports facilities. We understand the need for quick setup before games and fast removal after tournaments. We can place units near each field or court for maximum convenience.
