ADA Compliance Requirements

Understanding ADA Porta Potty Requirements

The Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) sets specific requirements for accessible portable toilets:

  • For Events: Events with more than 500 attendees must provide at least one accessible porta potty. For events with fewer attendees, at least 2% of total portable toilets must be ADA compliant.
  • For Construction Sites: OSHA requires employers to provide accessible restroom facilities for workers with disabilities when other facilities are not readily accessible.
  • Unit Requirements: ADA compliant units must have: door width of at least 32 inches, toilet seat height of 17-19 inches, grab bars on both sides, adequate turning space inside (60-inch diameter circle).
  • Placement: ADA accessible units should be placed on level ground and have an accessible path leading to them.
