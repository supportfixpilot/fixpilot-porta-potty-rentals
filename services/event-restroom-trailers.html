Why Choose Event Restroom Trailers?

Here's why event planners prefer restroom trailers over standard porta potties:

  • Guest Experience: Trailers provide a much more comfortable experience for event guests.
  • Higher Capacity: Multiple stalls mean shorter lines and happier guests.
  • Climate Control: Keep guests comfortable in any weather.
  • Professional Appearance: Trailers look better at upscale events.
  • Better Amenities: Running water, proper lighting, and premium features.

How Many Restroom Trailers Do You Need?

Choosing the right number of restroom trailers is crucial for guest satisfaction:

  • 50-100 guests: 1 small trailer (2-4 stalls) recommended
  • 100-250 guests: 1 medium trailer (4-6 stalls) recommended
  • 250-500 guests: 1-2 large trailers (6-8 stalls) recommended
  • 500+ guests: Multiple large trailers or combination units

For events serving alcohol, increase capacity by 20-30% as restroom usage increases. Our team can help calculate the perfect number for your specific event.
