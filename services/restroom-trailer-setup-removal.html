Professional Restroom Trailer Setup & Removal Services

Need professional restroom trailer setup and removal? FixPilot handles everything from delivery to installation to final pickup. Our professional setup services ensure your trailer is properly positioned, level, and ready for use - and we remove everything when you're done.

Our setup and removal services take the hassle out of renting restroom trailers. We deliver the trailer to your location, professionally set it up (including any necessary connections), and when your rental period ends, we return to disconnect, clean, and remove the trailer. You don't have to worry about a thing!

At FixPilot, we believe great service doesn't end with delivery. Our professional team ensures your trailer is properly installed and will return to remove it when you're done. This complete service is included in your rental price - no hidden fees or surprises.
