Irving Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Dallas County and City of Irving regulations is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) in Irving generally do not require a street permit.
  • Right-of-Way Permits: If placing a unit on an Irving public sidewalk, street, or alleyway, a temporary right-of-way permit from the City is mandatory.
