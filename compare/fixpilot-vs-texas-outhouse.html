FixPilot vs Texas Outhouse — Texas regional vs national

By Jordan Reed· Reviewed by Maria Alvarez· Updated 2026-06-12

Texas Outhouse is a long-established Houston-based porta-potty operator with deep roots across the Gulf Coast and East Texas. They've served Houston-area construction and oilfield customers for decades. We're a national operator with our own depots in the same Texas metros — here's when each makes sense.

The 30-second answer

Pick Texas Outhouse if you're a Houston-metro customer with existing relationships and your projects stay within their core service area. Pick FixPilot for multi-state portfolios spanning Texas and other regions, federal-procurement contracting, and one-call dispatch across all 36 of our Texas cities plus the rest of the country.

Or call us at (833) 652-9344 — we’ll tell you if Texas Outhouse is the better fit.

Where each company is strong

Texas Outhouse’s home turf: Deep Houston-metro density, decades of construction-industry relationships, oilfield experience along the Gulf Coast, established Texas regional brand.

FixPilot’s coverage: Multi-state customer support for portfolios that span Texas and other states. Phone-first lead intake. Same-day delivery in 36 Texas cities (the most-covered state in our network). SAM.gov registered for federal procurement.
