Sound Transit's Northgate Link light rail extension and the planned Lynnwood Link extension through Shoreline and Mountlake Terrace add public infrastructure construction throughout King County. Seattle's residential construction — ADU construction in Ballard, Crown Hill, and Greenwood responding to the city's upzoning program — keeps residential contractors active throughout the city's northern neighborhoods.
