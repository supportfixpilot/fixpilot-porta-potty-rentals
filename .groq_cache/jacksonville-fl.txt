Naval Station Mayport's fleet maintenance facility upgrades, Naval Air Station Jacksonville's construction programs, and the Cecil Commerce Center's Amazon and Volkswagen Atlas production expansion add military and industrial construction to Duval County's pipeline. The Jacksonville Port Authority's JAXPORT dredging program creating 47-foot channels for post-Panamax vessels has triggered adjacent container terminal infrastructure construction in the Dames Point and Blount Island areas. Riverside Avenue's historic commercial corridor renovation, San Marco's mixed-use development along Hendricks Avenue, and the suburban residential construction explosion in Nocatee and Ponte Vedra Beach in St. Johns County add commercial and residential volume. Jacksonville's event market reflects its unique position as Florida's largest city by land area and a major military community. The Jacksonville Landing riverfront — rebuilt after fire — and Metropolitan Park host the Jacksonville Jazz Festival drawing 300,000 over Memorial Day weekend. TaxSlayer Gator Bowl at EverBank Stadium, the Jacksonville Jaguars NFL season, and the Florida-Georgia football game (annually the largest outdoor cocktail party in sports) draw massive crowds to the EverBank Stadium corridor each fall. The Greater Jacksonville Agricultural Fair, the Bank of America Riverwalk along the St. Johns, and the annual Gate River Run — one of the largest 15K races in the United States — complete a calendar that keeps Jacksonville's event sanitation demand active year-round. Outdoor wedding venues at TPC Sawgrass in Ponte Vedra Beach, Bolles School campus, and along the Intracoastal Waterway make the luxury restroom trailer market robust.
