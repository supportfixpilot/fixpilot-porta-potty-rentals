St. Louis County's Clayton office district redevelopment, the Maryland Heights industrial corridor's Amazon and FedEx distribution center expansions, and the Chesterfield Valley commercial corridor's continued growth add suburban construction volume. BJC HealthCare's Barnes-Jewish Hospital and Children's Hospital campus expansion in the Central West End — one of the largest academic medical center construction programs in the Midwest — keeps healthcare construction contractors continuously active.
