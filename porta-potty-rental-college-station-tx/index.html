College Station Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating Brazos County and municipal regulations is critical to avoid fines. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards) do not require a street permit.
  • Right-of-Way Permits: If placing a unit on a public sidewalk, street, or alleyway in Bryan or College Station, a temporary right-of-way permit is mandatory.
