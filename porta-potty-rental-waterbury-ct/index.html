Waterbury Placement & Compliance Rules

Navigating local regulations in New Haven County is critical to avoid fines and project delays. Our logistics team handles the placement strategy to keep your site 100% legal.

  • Private Property: Units placed entirely within your property line (driveways, fenced yards in Town Plot or East End) do not require a street permit.
  • Right-of-Way Permits: If placing a unit on a Waterbury public sidewalk, street, or alleyway, a temporary right-of-way permit from the City Public Works is mandatory.
